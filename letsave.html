Letsave

Building on a student project brief for a money-saving app, I created the overall brand positioning in a brand guidelines document. I then worked through the UX/UI design process. I developed key user stories/user flow diagrams, low/mid fidelity wireframes, user testing and iteration before designing the high fidelity wireframes and prototypes.

My Process

Ideation

Utilizing the user stories from the project brief, I developed the overall user flow diagram for the MVP.

Wireframes

I developed low and mid fidelity wireframes for a key user flow; adding funds to an existing goal.

Design

After I defined how to position the product, I began to develop its visual identity in the form of a brand guidelines document. Here I established the logo, color scheme, typography, and imagery.

User Testing

Using the mid fidelity wireframes, I conducted user testing with three participants and watched their interaction through screen-casting. Using the pain points gathered through my analysis I iterated on the existing design for the high fidelity mockups.

Prototype

Taking the user feedback into cosideration, and iterating on the existing wireframes, I developed the high fidelity screens.
